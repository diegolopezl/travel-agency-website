About Osaka

大阪市について

Osaka is a bustling metropolis in the heart of Japan's Kansai region. Known for its lively atmosphere, street food culture, and historic sites, Osaka is a city that showcases the country's rich history and modernity. It is home to several famous landmarks, including Osaka Castle, which was built in the late 16th century and is now one of the city's most popular tourist attractions. Osaka is also famous for its delicious cuisine, such as takoyaki (octopus dumplings) and okonomiyaki (Japanese pancakes), as well as its shopping and entertainment districts.
